Tiger Moth Academy

Take to the skies above Yorkshire in a vintage Tiger Moth. Take control of the aircraft and fly as the early aviators did in a trial lesson and experience the excitement of 1930s style flying. On the day, arrive at the airfield and receive a preflight briefing from our experienced pilot, dress in our replica flying suits/jackets, and take off.

Flights

Flights are from Breighton Airfield, Selby, YO8 6DS. A half-hour flight might typically pass over York Minster and the surrounding countryside. A full hour allows a trip out to the Humber bridge. Flight plans may be personalised if discussed in advance. Family and friends are welcome to watch your takeoff and landing from the comfort of our newly-built clubhouse, which serves hot and cold refreshments.
